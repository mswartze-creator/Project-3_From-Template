Story 2: The Coffee Date

My neighbor in my first apartment left a post-it note with his number on my front door. I accepted his coffee date offer, and spent 45 minutes listening to him explain that he was a 27-year-old night-shift prison guard who had used every minute of his free time in the past 2 years to read the Lord of the Rings over and over again. Nothing else. He fit every identifier of a person you really shouldn't be alone with.
